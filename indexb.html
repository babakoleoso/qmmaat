Python Temple • Python House

Sacred images from the Python House — a living temple where royal pythons are revered, protected, and honored as ancestral and cosmological beings.

Cultural & Spiritual Note:
In Benin and across parts of West Africa, the royal python is regarded as sacred—often associated with continuity, protection, and ancestral presence. The Python House is a consecrated sanctuary where pythons are cared for with reverence and responsibility. These images are shared to honor tradition, lineage, and sacred stewardship.
